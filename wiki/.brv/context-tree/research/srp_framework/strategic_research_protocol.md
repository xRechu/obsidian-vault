## Narrative
### Structure
The SRP framework decomposes market research into five distinct phases: Seed, Discovery, Audit, Gap Analysis, and Automation.

### Highlights
Employs a 5-pillar Vibe Extraction Audit to analyze competitors. Focuses on identifying "patterns of sameness" to find unique market gaps.

### Rules
1. Contextual Seed: Define 2-3 brands and niche keywords.
2. Multi-Layer Discovery: Use semantic expansion and ad-library scraping.
3. Vibe Extraction: Analyze Hook, Palette, Pacing, Narrative, and Engagement.
4. Synthesis: Identify the "Sea of Sameness" vs. the "Pattern Interrupt".
5. Automation: Save to /research/ and feed into Strategic Vibe Matrix.
